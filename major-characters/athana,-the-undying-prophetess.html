Loyal to no city, but rather to the whole of Atlantis.
Prophetess of Poseidon (defunct), and maintainer and keeper of all of Atlantis's cities.
Only maintaining and keeping all of Atlantis in order to await the reincarnation of Anagenn.
Meditates in the depths of a temple to Poseidon, continually channeling her mystic.

Mystic: Oceanus, the Father of Rivers and Oceanids

A shell- and gem-encrusted diadem colored in sea-turquoise. Small inscriptions of art regarding the myth of Oceanus are inscribed into it, noting rivers and Oceanids all over the diadem.
It appears to be several millennia old, though not any more fragile for it.

Personality: Oceanus

Somber, yet determined to guide Athana to the best of his ability, as a fellow ruler.

Rivers

  • Aqueduct
    • Water Control: You have immediate and absolute control over water in a 120 ft. radius from you, with no action required.
      A creature may attempt to wrest control of water in their body by making a Constitution save against your spell save DC.
      A creature may attempt to wrest control of water under their control by making an Intelligence save against your spell save DC.
      You may only contest a creature once per day.
    • Water is Life: Passive: You can support several city's worth of people by filtering saltwater to freshwater.
      Note: This includes: drinking, irrigation, bathing, and latrines.
    • Breath of the Sea: Passive: Be able to continually breathe underwater. Be able to stay awake as long as water in a range of 60ft. is moving. Gain 120ft. of swimming speed.
  • Flow
    • Like a Calm River: You alter perception around up to six creatures of your choice with a bonus action.
      Each target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be affected by this Art for the duration.
      An affected target's speed is halved, it takes a −4 penalty to AC and Dexterity saving throws, and it can't use reactions. On its turn, it can use either an action or a bonus action, not both. Regardless of the creature's abilities or magic items, it can't make more than one melee or ranged attack during its turn. If the creature attempts to cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action, roll a d20. On a 6 or higher, the spell doesn't take effect until the creature's next turn, and the creature must use its action on that turn to complete the spell. If it can't, the spell is wasted.
      A creature affected by this Art makes another Wisdom saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, the effect ends for it.
      You may use "Like a Calm River" {Proficiency Bonus} times per day.
    • As the Raging River: Choose a willing creature that you can see within range with a bonus action.
      Until the Art ends, the target's speed is doubled, it gains a +4 bonus to AC, it has advantage on Dexterity saving throws, and it gains an additional action on each of its turns. That action can be used only to take the Attack, Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use an Object action.
      You may use "As the Raging River" {Proficiency Bonus} times per day.
    • Like Water: You can transform a willing creature you touch, along with everything it's wearing and carrying, into a watery blob for the duration with an action and concentration.
      The Art ends if the creature drops to 0 hit points. An incorporeal creature isn't affected.
      While in this form, the target gains 100 temporary hitpoints. The target also gains a swimming speed of 120 feet, if its swimming speed is lower than 120 feet. The target has resistance to nonmagical damage, and it has advantage on Dexterity, and Constitution saving throws. The target can pass through small holes, narrow openings, and even mere cracks. The target is also invisible in water.
      Downside: While in the form of a watery blob, the target can't talk, and any objects it was carrying or holding can't be dropped, used, or otherwise interacted with. The target can't attack or cast spells or Arts with a verbal component.
      You may use "Like Water" once per long rest.
  • Rapids
    • Flash Flood: With an action, a massive globule of water pours from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range then explodes with a loud roar into a burst of water.
      Each creature in a 40-foot radius sphere centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell proficiency. A target takes 14d20 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
      The water spreads around corners. It extinguishes flaming objects in the area.
      You may use "Flash Flood" {Proficiency Bonus} times per short rest.
    • Watery Vortex: A 60-foot radius sphere of whirling water bursts into existence, centered on a point you choose within range with an action. The sphere remains for the Art's duration. The sphere's space is difficult terrain.
      Each creature within or entering into the sphere must make a Strength saving throw or take 8d20 bludgeoning damage.
      Until the Art ends, you can use a bonus action on each of your turns to cause a rapid of water to leap from the center of the sphere toward one creature you choose within 180 feet of the center. Make a ranged spell attack. You have advantage on the attack roll if the target is in the sphere. On a hit, the target takes 10d20 bludgeoning damage.
      You may use "Watery Vortex" {Proficiency Bonus} times per short rest.
    • Rapid Channeling: With a bonus action or reaction, teleport to any location with water within 120ft.
      Downside: If this location cannot contain your Size, take 10d10 force damage, but be able to able to fit.

Oceanids

  • Spring of Sparkling: You can create up to {Proficiency Bonus} Springs of Sparkling using 30 minutes of concentration.
    A creature who drinks from a Spring of Sparkling gains the effects of a Short Rest. This may occur once per long rest, per creature.
    Furthermore, the creature which drank from the Spring of Sparkling gains a sparkling effect, which gives -2 to Dexterity (Stealth) checks and +1 to Charisma (Performance) checks.
  • Pond of Purity: You can create up to {Proficiency Bonus} Ponds of Purity using 30 minutes of concentration.
    A creature that is washed in a Pond of Purity is fully cleansed of minor poisons, dirtiness, and diseases, as though Lesser Restoration and Prestidigitation were used on it.
    Note: Evergrowth is not a disease.
  • Rain of Renewal: You can create a small rain cloud with an action, which stays above your head, {Proficiency Bonus} times per short rest.
    You may direct your rain cloud with a free action to select a creature within 60 feet of you to regain 60 hit points. This may only occur {Proficiency Bonus} times per cloud.
    Note: This can work underwater.

Subsumed (Upgrades)

  • Achelous, the Father of Sirens -> Siren Call
    Upgrades Aqueduct: Water Control
    You can contest a creature that has succeeded against your "Aqueduct: Water Control" by targeting that creature in sight with an action. The creature must contest your Water Control again.
    You may only use "Siren Call" {Proficiency Bonus} times per long rest.
  • Acheron, the River of Woe -> The Entrance to the Underworld
    Upgrades Rapids: Watery Vortex
    You can cause a creature to make a Strength saving throw against your "Rapids: Watery Vortex" by using two actions and concentration. If the creature succeeds, it is propelled safely outside the watery vortex in a direction you choose. If the creature fails, you may choose to send that creature downward, to a limit of 4000 feet.
    Upgrades Rapids: Rapid Channeling
    The range of "Rapids: Rapid Channeling" is increased to 360 ft., and the damage is reduced to 5d5 force damage.
  • Styx, the Dread River of Oath -> Dread Oath
    Upgrades Oceanids and Aqueduct: Water Control
    A contract written and signed between you and a party must be upheld. If that contract is broken, that creature cannot benefit from any of your mystic Arts, such as "Oceanids: Spring of Sparkling." In addition, that creature has disadvantage on their contest against "Aqueduct: Water Control."
  • Metis, the Mother of Wisdom -> "... like water."
    Upgrades Flow: Like Water
    You may choose to increase "Flow: Like Water" number of targets to two.
    Note: See Anagenn's Evergrowth Water Lily Petal: "Love is..."
Achelous, the Father of Sirens

A strange, blue-colored fin of a Bullfish Leviathan. It's inscribed with the myth of Achelous, noting that he had sired many sirens.
Siren Call: upgrades: Aqueduct: Water Control.

Acheron, the River of Woe

A floating mass of water that appears to enter and exit in and from the wielder's hair. The water occasionally twists, depicting Charon's ferry and the souls of the dead.
The Entrance to the Underworld: upgrades: Rapids: Watery Vortex and Rapids: Rapid Channeling.

Styx, the Dread River of Oath

An affixable tablet made of water with inscriptions of the local law. It appears to be resolute and deterministic, being unbendable and unbreakable if sworn upon by oath.
Dread Oath: upgrades: Oceanids and Aqueduct: Water Control.

Metis, the Mother of Wisdom

A captured sensation of age and wisdom; the solemnity of an ancient ruler... yet with a small taste of mercy and love.
"... like water.": upgrades: Flow: Like Water.

Note: the following Paths are subsumed.
*Achelous, the Father of Sirens*
Paths: Siren Call
- The Song of Sirens: With an action and short burst of song, cause all creatures within a 60ft. radius to contest a 15 DC Wisdom Save, or be Charmed, Blinded, and Paralyzed.
- Rage of the Divine Bull: **Passive**: Your Strength score is now 21.
- The Eldest and Honored River: You have immediate control over water in a 60ft. radius from you, with no action required. You do not control water contacted by a creature.

*Acheron, the River of Woe*
Paths: The Entrance to the Underworld
- Feature 1: The Entrance: With an action, and no Save, force a creature to sink up to a limit of 60ft.
- Feature 2: Ferryman: With an action, teleport to a place you can see within 60ft. with up to 5 other creatures.

*Styx, the Dread River of Oath*
Paths: Dread Oath
- Unbreakable Oath: An oath agreed to by two parties must be upheld. If this oath is broken, the breaker of the oath is inflicted with Petrification for one week, and both parties are notified of the oath's breaking.
- By the Oath: An oath agreed to by two parties must be upheld. If this oath is broken, the breaker of this oath has disadvantage on attacks against the other party, and the other party has advantage on attacks against the oath breaker.

*Metis, the Mother of Wisdom*
Paths: Hands of the Wise Maker
- The Wise Crafter: **Passive**: Gain 8 tool proficiencies of your choice.
- The Mother of Wisdom: **Passive**: Your Wisdom score is now 21.
- Motherly Love: **Passive**: When acting in love, your abilities are empowered.
