Appearance & Personality

A tall, confident man, with sun-kissed skin. He appears to be confident and proud, but not to the point of being overbearing. Often considered charismatic and friendly by family and friends. Perhaps considered unmannered and disrespectful.

Occupation

Currently loyal (if dissatisfied) to Kýmatlantis.
To be the diplomat to the "Outside World" is effectively a soft-exile, as a punishment for having fraternized with enemy cities.
Considers the job to be a bore, and instead surfs for most hours of the day.
Regardless, he does discusses the goings-on of Atlantis with the occasional Roman taxman and the rare traveller, which he counts as "diplomacy."

Evergrowth Scales

An unusual growth of prismatic scales taken from an Evergrowth that shined brightly, yet sickeningly, through an array of colors in the sunlit sea.

Juvenile Shark Leviathan's Gills

The gill and the accompanying organs that would have permitted a juvenile Shark Leviathan to eventually become an efficient predator of the depths.
Upgrades: Breathe.

Juvenile Shark Leviathan's Fins

The fins of a powerful Shark Leviathan, having once propelled a juvenile beast of the sea.
